The Variety You Deserve

Whether you are planning to buy indoor plants or outdoor plants, we have got you covered. Plantly brings a rich variety of plants for all your home decor needs.

Plantly has categorized plants into succulents, indoor plants, pet-friendly plants, and other categories to make buying choice easier for people looking to buy plants online in India.

For Customers who wish to buy plants but are not sure which plants to buy, we provide guidance and plantscaping services. With Plantly by your side, you never have to worry about finding the right plant.

All our plants come in varieties of premium planters, which makes a complete set ready to be added to your home decor or for that perfect gift you wish to give to your loved ones.

Premium Planters

Are you looking for the perfect premium potted plant for your home decor? Plantly brings to you premium handcrafted planters that are size-matched with every plant you buy.

All plants sold by Plantly are double-potted with a grow pot and a premium planter for decor. This ensures that all our plants have optimal air-flow and drainage. This arrangement makes the pots self-watering.

The planters are prepared in small batches by highly skilled artisans from India. These planters are durable and extremely easy to clean, so they remain new for years.

Plantly premium potted are a perfect gift to your loved ones. with every part of the product made by experts in India.

Comfort

By making your plant purchases online, you can avoid the hassle of going to actual nurseries, negotiating crowded aisles, and wasting time looking for particular plants. When you choose the online option, you can access the plant with just a few clicks and taps, which is much more convenient than doing your plant shopping offline.
